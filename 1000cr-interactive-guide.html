Market research
9 tasks · 7 AI-automatable
▾
Category sizing and TAM estimation AI
Competitor product audit (ingredients, pricing, claims) AI
Consumer sentiment mining (Reddit, Amazon, YouTube) AI
Google Trends and search demand mapping AI
White space identification (unmet needs matrix) AI
ICP definition and persona building Human
Pricing strategy and willingness-to-pay research Human
Regulatory landscape scan (FSSAI, DCGI, AYUSH) AI
Adjacent category opportunity mapping AI
AI playbook
Tools
Apify (scrape Reddit, Amazon, YouTube), Claude/GPT for synthesis, Google Trends API, SimilarWeb
Setup
Build 5 Apify scrapers (one per source). Feed all output into Claude with a structured brief. Set agents to re-run weekly.
Output
One-page category brief: TAM, top 10 competitors ranked, 3 white spaces, consumer language bank, regulatory flags.
Time saved
2-3 weeks of manual research compressed to 1 day. Recurring updates in minutes.

AI and knowledge OS
8 tasks · 4 AI-automatable
▾
Shared context layer (brand truths, ICP, positioning) Human
Skills library (reusable AI prompts that compound) AI
Decision log (every strategic call, dated, reversible) Human
Workflow automation (agents for recurring tasks) AI
Cross-functional knowledge sync AI
AI tool evaluation and stack management Human
Team onboarding via OS (new hire reads the OS, not a deck) AI
Compounding loop (feed wins/losses back into skills) Human
AI playbook
Tools
GitHub for the OS repo, Claude Code/Cowork for AI interface, Obsidian or Notion for notes, markdown files for skills and decision logs
Setup
Three layers. Slow layer: brand truths, audience research, frameworks (rarely changes). Medium layer: category plans, active positioning, market context (updated monthly). Fast layer: decision log with date, rationale, reversal criteria (updated daily). Every AI tool reads the OS before generating anything.
Output
Any team member's AI has the same context as the founder. New hires productive in days, not months. Skills compound: one person's discovery becomes everyone's baseline.
Key insight
This is the function that makes all other functions work with 5 people. Without it, you have 5 people using 15 tools with 15 different contexts. With it, you have a single operating brain that gets sharper every week.

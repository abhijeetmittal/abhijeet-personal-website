About

AI, health, and D2C, built in India.

I have spent years understanding Indian consumers and building brands for them, then turning what works into systems a small team can run.

I am deeply into health and spend significant time understanding nutrition science, longevity, and how the body responds to exercise, food, sleep, and supplements. I research health, AI, and brand-building extensively, then apply it in my day-to-day work.

I have played tennis for 15 years and run 10Ks. I can play most Indian sports. I perform at a high level on the court, on the track, and at work. That is just how I am wired.

AI has changed how I work in the last two years. In market research, category strategy, or consumer behaviour, I use it deeply. I also mentor startup founders and operators on brand-building, D2C strategy, and navigating the early stages of building something from scratch.
